Link, Hero Of Time

The Legendary Hero, Link, is a mystery to most. His origin is usually just as vague. Those who encounter him, know him only as the 'Hero Of Time.' He is always there whenever a threat to life comes. His various incarnations through the land of Hyrule always seem to be right when he is needed most, and whenever a princess needs saving.

Abilities

Link, has incredible stregnth. He is known to lift more than 4 times his body weight. He has vast knowledge of swordsmanship and other tools, and can learn new abilities on the fly. He is extremely agile and capable of feats of speed not normal to just any human. He is often imbued with the power of the Tri-force of Courage.

Work History

Links main occupation is saving the kingdom of Hyrule and its surrounding villages. He defeats Ganon whenever possible and saves princess Zelda. He also makes a habit of tending to the needs of helpless citizens. Everything from catching ghosts, to gathering chickens.
